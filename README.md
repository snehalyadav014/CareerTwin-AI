🚀 CareerTwin AI – AI Career Intelligence & Placement Management Agent

🌟 Overview

CareerTwin AI is an enterprise-grade, AI-powered Career Intelligence and Placement Management Platform designed to transform how students prepare for placements and how institutions manage placement readiness.

In today's competitive job market, students often struggle to understand their career readiness, identify skill gaps, choose the right learning path, and align themselves with industry requirements. CareerTwin AI addresses these challenges by providing intelligent insights, personalized recommendations, and data-driven career guidance through a unified platform.

The solution empowers students to upload resumes, assess placement readiness, discover missing skills, generate learning roadmaps, and receive AI-driven career recommendations. Simultaneously, it enables placement cells and training departments to gain complete visibility into student performance, readiness trends, and placement analytics.
